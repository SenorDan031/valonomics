# Guidelines for Team Discipline

- All team members must maintain harmony and work in coordination with one another.
- Clear communication is mandatory. Members should openly share doubts, ideas, or issues in a respectful manner.
- Any form of nuisance or misconduct will not be tolerated. If a member is reported more than five times and the accusation is verified, that member will be removed from the project and permanently revoked from access.
- Senior members are expected to assist newcomers and ensure a welcoming environment for new contributors.
- Assigned tasks must be completed within the given time frame. In case of major technical issues, reasonable extensions may be granted.
- Maintain a positive and collaborative work environment. This project is driven by passion for development, so work responsibly while enjoying the process.

## Guidelines for Data Entry in Team Assets

- Weekly or monthly meetings must be recorded and shared via accessible links (Google Drive, YouTube, etc.).
- All flowcharts and architectural diagrams must be uploaded exclusively to the designated folders.
- Content must follow the predefined structure:

  - Meeting videos must be documented inside `videoLinks.md`, including:
    - Date  
    - Topic of discussion  
    - Host name  

  - Flowcharts must be placed inside the `Charts/` directory.

- Any alteration to established formats without prior approval may result in irreversible consequences.
